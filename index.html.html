01 // About

The analyst behind the case file

I work at the intersection of risk, policy, and data — reviewing rider and driver activity on Uber's platform to catch fraud before it becomes a loss. My days are spent digging through operational tools, verifying stakeholder accounts, and making fair, policy-compliant fare adjustments under tight SLA windows.

What got me promoted from Associate Analyst to Analyst within a year wasn't luck — it was consistency: high accuracy, careful root-cause analysis, and a refusal to let a case go through half-checked.
